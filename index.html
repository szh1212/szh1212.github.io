Internship Experience

OpenArm Robotic Arm Prototype Reproduction

Internship Project · Jul. 2025 – Aug. 2025

Participated in the reproduction of a 7-DoF OpenArm left-arm prototype, covering the full workflow from mechanical design and hardware assembly to software configuration, system integration, and debugging.

  • Completed STL-to-STEP conversion, engineering drawings, and iterative structural replacement from 3D-printed parts to sheet-metal/CNC components.
  • Fabricated wiring harnesses, configured CAN IDs for seven motors, and verified single-joint communication and motion control.
  • Built the Ubuntu 22.04 + ROS2 Humble environment, validated SocketCAN communication, deployed URDF/RViz visualization, and tested MoveIt2 simulation.
  • Implemented preliminary command-line motor control and explored early-stage ROS2 position-control integration.

Reference: OpenArm: Open-source Robotic Arm Platform
